PSYCHOLOGIST

Introduction

Psychology is the study of the human mind and behavior. It explores topics like cognition, emotions, perception, personality, and interpersonal relationships. Understanding pyschological principles can provide insights into how people think, feel, and act. As professionals in the field, pyschologists work to assess, diagnose, and treat mental, emotional, and behavioral issues. Their roles may involve research, providing therapy, administering tests, or consulting with organizations on human behavior and performances. Pyschologists apply their expertise across various settings like clinics, schools, businesses, and researching institutions.
